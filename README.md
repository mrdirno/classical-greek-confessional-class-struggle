## The Story

In the shadow of the Acropolis, a young enforcer learns that in Athens, every man is either a creditor or a slave.

## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a chapter from the perspective of the fish-sauce merchant's daughter, exploring the illness and the terror of her father's debt.
- Draft a monologue by Philon the Lender explaining his economic philosophy to a skeptical Athenian aristocrat.
- Create a scene where Brasidas reflects on his own past and why he enforces the system that enslaved him.
- Write a 'deleted scene' showing a conversation between Xanthias and the slave girl at the well, one year after the novel's events.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive 'Debt Web' visualization showing how characters in the novel are financially and socially entangled.
- Design a text-based game where the player, as Xanthias, must make moral choices in debt collection, with consequences for loyalty, wealth, and freedom.
